    Bus

    Pune's bus service, operated by Pune Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal Limited (PMPML), is the backbone of public transportation in the city. With an extensive network that spans urban and suburban areas, PMPML buses are a reliable and economical choice for daily commuters. The fleet includes both non-AC and AC buses, providing a range of comfort levels to suit different needs. Buses are well-suited for longer commutes across the city, connecting major commercial, residential, and educational zones.

    The Pune Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal Limited (PMPML) operates an extensive bus network that connects the city's major hubs and suburbs. The buses are generally reliable, affordable, and run on fixed routes and schedules.

    Auto Rickshaw

    Auto rickshaws are a ubiquitous sight on Pune's roads and offer a flexible and quick way to navigate the city. These three-wheeled vehicles are ideal for short to medium distances, especially in congested areas where larger vehicles might struggle. Fares are typically metered, but it's common to negotiate prices, particularly for longer journeys or during peak hours. Auto rickshaws are a go-to option for spontaneous and efficient travel within the city.

    Metro

    The Pune Metro is a significant addition to the city’s transport infrastructure, designed to ease traffic congestion and provide a fast, reliable mode of transit. Although still expanding, the metro promises to connect major parts of the city with state-of-the-art trains and modern stations. Once fully operational, it will be a key mode of transport for daily commuters, offering a comfortable and speedy alternative to road travel.

    Taxi

    Taxis are available throughout Pune and can be hailed via various mobile apps. They offer a comfortable and private way to travel.
    Taxis in Pune, including app-based ride-sharing services like Uber and Ola, offer the convenience of door-to-door travel. These services are especially useful for those who prefer comfort and privacy, as well as for longer trips, airport transfers, or late-night journeys. With the ease of booking through mobile apps and availability of various vehicle options, taxis provide a reliable and hassle-free transportation choice.

    Bicycle

    Bicycles are a sustainable and healthy way to explore Pune. Several bike-sharing services are available across the city.
    Cycling is gaining popularity in Pune as a sustainable and healthy mode of transport. The city has been developing cycling tracks and promoting bike-sharing programs to encourage this eco-friendly option. Bicycles are perfect for short commutes, leisurely rides, or exploring scenic areas of Pune. The increasing availability of rental bicycles also makes it easy for residents and visitors to adopt this green mode of transportation.

    Train

    Trains are a crucial part of Pune's transportation network, connecting the city to nearby towns and cities.
    Pune's railway station is a major hub, connecting the city to various parts of India. Trains are a preferred option for long-distance travel, offering different classes of service to suit varying comfort levels and budgets. For intra-city travel, suburban trains are available but are less prominent compared to buses and other modes. The railway network is essential for those commuting to nearby cities or traveling across the country.

    Plane

    Pune International Airport, located in Lohegaon, serves as the primary air travel hub for the city. The airport connects Pune to major domestic destinations and several international locations. Air travel is the fastest mode of transport for long-distance journeys and is especially convenient for business travelers and tourists visiting Pune from distant regions. The airport is well-connected to the city by various transport options, including taxis and buses.

    Electric Rickshaw

    Electric Rickshaws are a new and eco-friendly transportation option available in Pune, offering a green alternative to traditional autos.
    Electric rickshaws, or e-rickshaws, are an emerging and eco-friendly mode of transport in Pune. These battery-powered vehicles offer a quiet, zero-emission alternative to traditional auto rickshaws. E-rickshaws are gaining popularity for short trips within residential areas and market zones. They provide a sustainable option for urban commuting, contributing to reduced pollution levels in the city.

    Rental Bikes

    Rental bikes are available at various locations in Pune. They offer flexibility and convenience for short trips within the city.
    Rental bikes are a popular choice for those who prefer the flexibility and convenience of two-wheeler travel without owning a vehicle. Several rental services offer motorbikes and scooters on an hourly or daily basis, making it easy for residents and visitors to navigate Pune’s streets. Rental bikes are particularly advantageous for tourists or anyone needing quick, independent travel across the city.

    Car Rentals

    Car rental services are available for those who prefer driving themselves. Various providers offer cars for rent on an hourly or daily basis.
    Car rentals provide a convenient option for those who prefer the comfort and control of driving their own vehicle while in Pune. Whether for a day trip, business travel, or exploring the city at your own pace, rental cars offer flexibility and privacy. Various rental agencies provide a range of vehicles, from budget-friendly options to luxury cars, catering to different needs and preferences.
